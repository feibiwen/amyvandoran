Why use a Matchmaker?

Amy Van Doran is renowned as the hippest matchmaker in New York. At AVD Consulting, we work with some of the most amazing men and women in New York City. Dating in New York City can be difficult and racked with confusion. In a city filled with millions of fabulous eligibles, we help you find your dream match and fit love back into your life!
