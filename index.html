Why use a matchmaker?

At AVD Consulting, Amy Van Doran is single-handedly changing the antiquated ideology of matchmaking while fighting against the unrealistic portrayals on television. We don't discriminate against age or gender. We work with the most amazing men and women. Successful, creative, bohemian types who are too high profile to online date, or too busy to weed out the good dates from the bad dates. Amy adores all of her clients, and has exquisite taste in people for the dates.

With Amy Van Doran and her team, we understand that dating in New York city is difficult and tiresome. In a city of millions, we help you find your dream match and fit love back into your life.
