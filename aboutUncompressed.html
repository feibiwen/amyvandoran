Amy Van Doran

CEO/Matchmaker

About Amy

Amy Van Doran genuinely cares about your love life. Known to many as the coolest matchmaker in New York City, she lives, breathes, and sweats romance. Over a hundred clients have successfully placed their love lives into Amy's hands, with stupendous results. Amy does it all, from the introductions to the officiating of weddings! Before she found her true calling as the "guru of all things love," she acted in a lot of movies, dressed a lot of people, and made some really weird art.

Almost nothing gives Emily a bigger thrill than figuring out what makes people tick- and what makes people click. She wants to know everything about you: your motivations, hopes, dreams, and what, when you get right down to it, makes you different from everybody else. The only thing she likes better? Finding someone who is as unique and special as you are and bringing the two of you together.

About Francesca

Francesca helps extraordinary people who happen to be single to find the loving relationships they are seeking. Francesca is a regular love expert on The Today Show with Kathie Lee and Hoda, The Hoda Show on SiriusXM and is a contributor to multiple websites, including The Huffington Post.
