ONE ON ONE MATCHING

The client-matchmaker relationship is a dynamic process which involves you learning more about yourself, breaking any bad dating habits, and meeting some of the most amazing likeminded relationship-oriented people in NYC. We pre-screen individuals in our midtown office and present the most qualified matches for you to vet. We provide ongoing feedback, lots of love, and occasionally uncork a bubbly in the office.

MATCHMAKING EVENTS

Amy Van Doran personally curates a fun demographically-minded events filled with an amazing crowd of hip, single, individuals looking to mingle. We regularly team up with start-ups, nonprofits, and cool bars for our parties. In the past, we have played Cupid, hosted parlor games at masquerade balls, co-hosted a fundraiser for children in Africa, and arranged beer-tastings for 30+, 40+ and 50+ crowds. Follow us on Facebook, Twitter or subscribe to our newsletter to find out when our next event will be!
